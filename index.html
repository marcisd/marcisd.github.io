All Aboard: Alphabet and early reading app

All Aboard

Unity EdTech Team Lead at All Aboard Learning

All Aboard Learning provides gamified EdTech solutions for struggling readers and spellers. They developed a visual phonics process called the Pictophonics as an aid for readers to decode words with visual guidance.

All Aboard is a complete systematic synthetic phonics (SSP) teaching tool, designed by teachers to make phonics sessions easier to deliver and progress more consistent across every class.

Key Features

Reading-Based Games. A plethora of fun minigames and activities that is structured around letter recognition, phoneme sound recognition, and reading and segmenting words.

Regionalized Curricula. Designed to work in harmony with different dialects and with different school system requirements around the world.

Decodable Ebook Library. A complete set of illustrated short decodable ebooks that integrate with the curriculum.

Learner Management System. A dashboard for parent and teacher facilitators to manage and monitor the learners and groups in their accounts.

Other Features

Text-To-Speech Synthesis. Convert texts into lifelike sounding speech using Google Cloud Text-To-Speech.

Experience API. Uses the standardized data collection specification xAPI. This allows for consistent data about learning experiences throughout a range of learning activities.

Crisalix 4D Augmented Reality Live VR

Crisalix

3D Software Developer at Crisalix

Crisalix uses artificial intelligence, computer vision, and augmented reality in the creation of 3D reconstructions of human bodies and the simulation of aesthetic procedures on top.

Crisalix is a preoperative 3D simulation service that enables plastic surgeons to increase their consult-to-convertion rate. It allows patients to make better informed preoperative choices for the surgical methods and enables surgeons to precisely plan the procedures.

Key Contributions

Scars Simulation. This feature allows the user to do a "freehand drawing" of scars into the patient's simulation. It also provides a simulation on how the scar should heal over time.

Mirroring Tool. This feature is used for reconstructive procedures where one half of the body is used as reference to restore or correct deformities on the other half of the body. It is also used to simulate asymmetry correction procedures.

Face Implants. This feature allows the user to select from a catalog of forhead, nose, chin, and cheek implants. The selected implants can be applied to the patient's simulation and adjusted until the desired outcome is achieved.

KidZania Manila x XS Multimedia

KidZania Manila x XS Multimedia

Game Production Consultant at XS Multimedia

KidZania is an educational entertainment theme park for children aged 4 to 17. Kids get to role-play different adult activities and earn currency in a realistic kid-sized city.

KidZania Manila partnered with First Gen, one of the leading providers of clean and renewable power in the Philippines, to launch the play-activity area called the First Gen Clean Energy Center.

As the Clean Energy Engineers, the kids use an interactive database that will help them discover the science behind generating clean and renewable electricity so they can decide on the best source of energy to power the kid-sized city.

Marine Research Lab

KidZania Manila partnered with the World Wildlife Fund for Nature (WWF) to open a new play-activity area dubbed the Marine Research Lab.

In this mock underwater facility, kids learn about marine ecosystems and how to care for it. They use an interactive database that showcases the marine biodiversity in the different locations around the Philippines and the threats they face.

🕶 Tech Demo

SameGame Demo

I chose the game SameGame for this demo because of its simple game design and simple architecture. It was also an opportunity for me to revisit an old project and explore how I can expand my knowledge of artificial intelligence and how it is used generate adaptive, responsive, and intelligent game simulations.

The Monte Carlo Tree Search algorithm is used to explore an approximation of the best possible moves. The Greedy Best-First Search heuristics is used in expanding towards most promising moves.

Custom 2D Orthogonal Tile Map in Unity

Inspired by a lot of 2D indie games as well as the classic ones, I did an exploration on the implementation tile-based graphics in Unity. This project also explores the use of auto-tiling or the technique of automating the drawing of tile graphics based on its surroundings.

This was made prior to the addition of Unity's Tile Map package that was introduced in Unity version 2017.2.
